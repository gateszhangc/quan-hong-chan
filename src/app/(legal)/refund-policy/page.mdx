# Refund Policy for EasyClaw

## Our Commitment
At EasyClaw, we want you to be satisfied with your purchase. This policy explains refund eligibility for subscription and one-time purchases.

## Subscription Refunds

### Eligible for Refund
You may request a refund within 7 days of your initial subscription purchase if:
- You have used less than 20% of your included deployment quota
- Persistent technical issues prevent normal deployment and our support team cannot resolve them
- The service does not function as described on our website

### Not Eligible for Refund
Refunds are not available when:
- More than 7 days have passed since purchase
- More than 20% of included deployment quota has been used
- The charge is a renewal payment (you can cancel before renewal)
- The account is suspended for Terms violations
- The request is only a change of mind after material usage

## How to Request a Refund
1. Contact Support: Email support@easyclaw.pro with the subject line "Refund Request"
2. Include Details: Add account email, plan name, purchase date, and reason
3. Review Timeline: We review requests and respond within 3 business days
4. Refund Method: Approved refunds are returned to the original payment method, typically within 5-10 business days

## Subscription Cancellation
- You can cancel from your billing portal at any time
- Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing cycle
- You retain access until the paid period ends
- No partial refunds are provided for mid-cycle cancellation

## One-Time Purchases
- One-time purchases are non-refundable after material usage
- Unused one-time purchases may be eligible for refund within 7 days

## Chargebacks
Before filing a chargeback, contact support first so we can resolve the issue quickly. Unauthorized chargebacks may result in account suspension.

## Changes to This Policy
We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

## Contact Us
For refund questions, contact:
- **Email**: support@easyclaw.pro
- **Response Time**: within 3 business days

By purchasing from EasyClaw, you acknowledge that you have read and agree to this Refund Policy.

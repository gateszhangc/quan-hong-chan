# Privacy Policy for Hormuz Strait News

## Overview
Hormuz Strait News is an informational website focused on public reporting, context, and analysis related to the Strait of Hormuz, shipping risk, and energy-market transmission.

This Privacy Policy explains what information may be collected when you visit the site, why it may be collected, and how it may be used.

## Information We May Collect

### 1. Basic technical information
Like most websites, we may receive standard request data such as:
- IP address
- Browser and device type
- Operating system
- Referring page
- Requested URL
- Approximate region inferred from network data

### 2. Analytics and site-performance data
We may use analytics and measurement tools to understand:
- page views
- on-site navigation patterns
- referral sources
- device categories
- performance problems
- broken pages or interaction issues

These tools may use cookies or similar technologies.

### 3. Contact information you choose to provide
If you contact the site operator, we may receive the information you send, such as your name, email address, and message contents.

### 4. Public-source attribution data
When the site references public reporting, source URLs, publication names, timestamps, and related metadata may be stored or processed to organize editorial content.

## How We Use Information
We may use collected information to:
- operate, maintain, and secure the website
- understand readership and improve site usability
- measure site quality and diagnose technical issues
- review traffic patterns and editorial demand
- respond to contact requests
- prevent abuse, fraud, or malicious activity
- comply with legal obligations

## Cookies and Similar Technologies
The site may use cookies, local storage, or similar technologies for:
- analytics
- language or interface preferences
- stability and security

You can manage cookies through your browser settings. Disabling some cookies may affect how parts of the site behave.

## Sharing and Disclosure
We do not sell personal information collected through the site.

We may share limited information only when reasonably necessary with:
- hosting and infrastructure providers
- analytics or monitoring providers
- security and anti-abuse service providers
- legal authorities when required by law

## Data Retention
We retain information only for as long as reasonably necessary for:
- site operations
- analytics and performance review
- security investigation
- legal compliance

Retention periods may vary depending on the type of record involved.

## External Links and Sources
This website links to external publishers and public-source materials. Those third-party websites have their own privacy practices, and this policy does not govern them.

## Children's Privacy
The site is not directed to children under 13, and we do not intentionally collect personal information from children through the public news site.

## Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy as the website, analytics setup, or legal requirements change. The current version will always be posted on this page.

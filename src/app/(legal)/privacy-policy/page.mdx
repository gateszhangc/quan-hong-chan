# Privacy Policy for EasyClaw

## Introduction
Welcome to **EasyClaw**, a service for deploying and managing OpenClaw assistants. This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how you can control it.

## Information We Collect
We collect only what is necessary to provide and improve the service:

1. **Account Information**
   - Name, email, and authentication details used to create and secure your account.

2. **Deployment Configuration Data**
   - Settings you provide to deploy OpenClaw (for example: selected model, selected channel, environment settings).

3. **Integration Credentials**
   - Channel credentials you provide (for example: Telegram bot tokens). Sensitive secrets are encrypted in storage.

4. **Operational and Usage Data**
   - Deployment events, status updates, logs, timestamps, error reports, and reliability metrics.

5. **Device and Technical Data**
   - Browser type, device type, operating system, screen size, and IP-derived region for performance and security.

6. **Cookies and Similar Technologies**
   - Session cookies and preference cookies to keep you signed in and remember settings.

7. **Payment Information**
   - Billing details handled by our payment providers (for example: card brand, last four digits, billing email). We do not store full card numbers.

## How We Use Your Information
- Provide, operate, and improve EasyClaw and OpenClaw deployment workflows.
- Create and manage deployments according to your configuration.
- Authenticate users, prevent abuse, and secure the platform.
- Provide support, account notifications, and critical service updates.
- Comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms.

## Data Storage and Retention
- Deployment metadata and related operational logs are stored to support monitoring, troubleshooting, and account history.
- Integration credentials are encrypted and retained only as needed for active deployments.
- Billing records are retained as required for accounting, fraud prevention, and compliance.
- You may request deletion of your account data, subject to legal and contractual retention requirements.

## Sharing and Disclosure
We do **not** sell your personal data. We share data only with:
- **Service providers** that help operate EasyClaw (for example: hosting, storage, payments) under confidentiality and data-processing obligations.
- **Legal authorities** when disclosure is required by law, court order, or to protect rights, safety, and platform integrity.

## Security
We use encryption in transit, encrypted secret storage, access controls, and monitoring to protect your data. No online service is completely secure; use strong account security practices and contact us if you suspect unauthorized access.

## Your Rights and Choices
- Access or update your account information.
- Delete deployments and associated configuration data.
- Request account deletion, subject to legal retention obligations.
- Opt out of marketing emails (service-critical emails may still be sent).
- Control cookies via browser settings.

## Children’s Privacy
EasyClaw is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age required by your region). Do not use the service if you are under the applicable age.

## Changes to This Policy
We may update this policy to reflect product, legal, or security changes. We will update the effective date and publish significant changes on this page.
